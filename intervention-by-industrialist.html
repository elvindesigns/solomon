INTERVENTION 'SUPPORT' BY OSISIOMA INDUSTRIALISTS!

It was a well attened meeting today as members of the Osisioma INDUSTRIALISTS came together at the premises of Chemlap Nigeria Ltd to show their support and appreciation to Hon Solomon Adaelu for his kind gesture in alleviating their sufferings through his INTERVENTION PROGAMME on the dilapidated Old ABA-OWERRI/ NNPC/EKEAKPARA Federal Road. The poor state of the road has resulted immensely in low productivity in the overall output of the Industries and as such has resulted in the mass retrenchment of their workforce.

The INDUSTRIALISTS recounted the huge losses they have recorded so far due to the poor state of the road and expressed their willingness to support in order to actualize the objective of Hon Adaelu in making sure that the roads are fully rehabilitated before the raining season; and however pleaded with him to also extend the road rehabilitation to the adjoining roads leading to some of the Companies in the Industrial Layout.

They also used the opportunity to request for Honorable's INTERVENTION as well in the areas of POWER supply from ENUGU ELECTRICITY DISTRIBUTION COMPANY (EEDC) which has become epileptic due to inability of the Electricity Company to buy enough POWER from the GENERATING PLANTS. This ugly situation has significantly reduced their productivity level as well as heavily increase the cost of production. They also expressed their displeasure over the inability of NNPC to properly manage their INDUSTRIAL WASTE which has contaminated all the under-ground water in and around OSISIOMA INDUSTRIAL LAYOUT.

In his response, Hon Adaelu explained that his INTERVENTION Programme on the road was born out of his desire to improve on the living standard of his Constituents and the Rural Roads Rehabilitation and Grading have already commenced in the three Local Government Areas of his Constituency as such all the adjoining roads along the major road will be rehabilited. He also emphasised on the need for the COMPANIES to improve on their Corporate Social Responsibilities to their host Communities and wondered why such important road should be allowed to deteriorate considering the number of Industries and Multi-nationals in the area.

He also promised to look into their plight as enumerated as soon National Assembly reconvenes. Truly, Osisioma Industrial Layout has the highest concentration of Industries in Aba and as such they pledged their support to Hon Adaelu for his initiative on the INTERVENTION PROGRAMME and further promised to contribute their quota to make sure that this laudable Programme becomes a success.
