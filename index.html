About

Engr. Solomon
Ezinwa Adaelu

Engr. Solomon Ezinwa O. Adaelu is a member of the federal House of Representatives, representing Obingwa, Osisoma Ngwa, and Ugwunagbo federal constituency. He doubles as the deputy Chairman, House committee on Maritime Safety, Education and Administration as well as a house Committee member on Public Petition.
