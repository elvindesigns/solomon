ALL THE ROADS IN AGALABA SECTION OF ABAYI FULLY GRADED AND REHABILITATED

  • Posted On: September 26, 2019
  • Posted By: Admin

DONE AND DUSTED!

It was all jubilation as the Road Rehabilitation and Grading Team concluded its activities in the AGALABA SECTION of Abayi. The Rural Road Rehabilitation and Grading programme which was kick started in Osisioma LGA by HRH Eze Eze Ukaegbu ( Iyi 1 of Abayi - Amaugwu Autonomous Community) on 7th January 2017, have today completed work on the whole of Agalaba Section of Abayi.

It is on record that HRH Eze Ukaegbu (who is the Traditional Ruler of Hon Adaelu) had the privilege and honour of launching this laudable Programme in Osisioma Ngwa LGA; and he however used the event to commend the thoughtfulness and wisdom of Hon Adaelu in the acquisition of the Earth Moving Equipment that will solve the poor road situation that has bedevilled his Community and Osisioma at large.

This particular Project is so significant that it has saved the residents thousands of naira they normally contribute every dry season for the grading of the roads. In fact, the residents were at the verge of making such contributions when the Road Rehabilitation Equipment was drafted into the area.

The Residents applauded the vision of Hon Solomon Adaelu on this Road Rehabilitation Exercise that have removed the financial burden that was levied on them -in order to make the roads accessible after the RAINING SEASON. In other words, such monies will be channelled into other pressing needs of their families.

The Roads include:-

  • Agalaba Road.
  • Ugo Street.
  • Obia Lane
  • Tony Street
  • Mgbajiaka Road
  • Ukwu-Mango Road
  • Celestial Lane
  • Green light Road etc

While expressing the delight of the Residents, Chief Akoma as well as Chief Obioma Ogwuma individually commended the effort of Hon Adaelu in his people-oriented programme that is targeted at improving the living standard of his Constituents.

We thank the members of the Project Team led by the Coordinator - Mazi Chinkata Esiaba (Chocho) for a job well done!!!!
