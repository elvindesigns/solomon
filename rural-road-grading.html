THE ALL YEAR RURAL ROADS GRADING \ REHABILITATION PROGRAM IN OBINGWA - OSISIOMA - UGWUNAGBO FED. CONSTITUENCY A TRIPATITE ARRANGEMENT.

The all year round rural roads grading/rehabilitation programme

The all year round rural roads grading/rehabilitation programme which was launched on Jan 4th 2016 by His Excellency Dr. Okezie Ikpeazu at Ngwa High school, Abayi Aba, is a programme designed to ameliorate the sufferings of rural dwellers and the people by ensuring that all the rural roads within the Fed. Constituency is accessible and motorable all year round. This lofty and laudable programme which is the first of its kind was initiated by Hon. Adaelu Solomon in compliance to the fulfillment of his campaign promises during the electioneering campaign, for which the people are excited and happy.

But sad enough a tiny small minds out of envy have read ulterior meanings about the programme other than the service to the people which is the intention . But the beauty of it all, is that right from the conception, Implementation and execution of this lofty programme the Governor Dr. Okezie Ikpeazu and Sen.Enyi Abaribe was in the know and were carried along. They were adequately informed and briefed at every stage and they wholeheartedly gave their blessings.

I must place on record that Hon. Adaelu Solomon is grateful for the support and advice the duo gave him. Suffice is to say that the success story of this programme will always be incomplete without the Abia State Governor and Sen.Enyi Harcourt Abaribe's contributions. The synergy have brought the success of the programme. While Engr.Adaelu Solomon bought these earth moving equipments; Sen.Enyi Abaribe is paying the salaries of the operators and workers; while the Governor Dr. Ikpeazu is paying for fueling and gas needs of the equipments.

There can never be a better Collaboration than this that exits among our elected representatives in the federal constituency . And the end result is the effective implementing of the rural roads grading/rehabilitation programme going on simultaneously in the three local government areas of our Federal constituency, which is to the Glory of God and to the benefit to mankind. To all those that have contributed in one way or the other be rest assured that Hon. Adaelu Solomon sees and is grateful and I say UNU JISIKE.
