The Launch of the Constituency Development Project of Hon. Solomon Sloddy Adaelu today at Ngwa High School Aba.

  • Posted On: January 4th, 2017
  • Posted By: Admin

Governor Okezie Ikpeazu was Special Guest at the launch of the Constituency Development Project of Hon. Solomon Sloddy Adaelu today at Ngwa High School Aba.

It was a great event and the people of Obingwa/Osisioma/Ugwunagbo Federal Constituency are happy. No such thing has ever happened before in our area nor indeed any other place that a serving legislator will buy huge earth moving equipment worth hundreds of millions of Naira and dedicate exclusively for the all year round grading of rural roads to his people.

The event which was chaired by the Vice Chancellor of the Abia State University attracted members of the National and State Assemblies, Senator Abaribe, Hon. Darlington Nwokocha of Isiala Ngwa North/Isiala Ngwa South Federal Constituency, Hon Uko Nkole of Arochukwu/Ohafia Federal Constituency.

Col Emeka Ananaba (Rtd), immediate past Deputy Governor of Abia State, Hon. Speaker of the Abia State House of Assembly, Rt. Hon. Barrister Chikwendu Kanu, immediate past Speaker, Rt. Hon Martins Azubuike, State Chairman of the PDP, Chief Johnson Onuigbo and members of the State Working Committee of the party, an array of traditional rulers from the three local governments, religious leaders, women and youth groups, etc.
