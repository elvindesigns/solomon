RURAL ROADS REHABILITATION AND GRADING UPDATE!!

Serious work is on-going now as the RURAL ROADS REHABILITATION AND GRADING PROGRAMME kicks off in Ward four (4) of Akanu Okpulor Autonomous Community, Obingwa. The programme which was flagged off by HRH Eze COJ Akalabu on the 3rd of April, 2017 has seen to the rehabilitation of Akanu-Okpulor/Itu-Ngwa road and many others which have not been accessible by motorist for many years now.

The people expressed so much gratitude to our able Representative, Hon Solomon Adaelu as most of the Roads have not been Rehabilitated for over fifteen(15) years!

As a matter of fact, the living standard of the people have already changed now they can easily move their farm produce to ready market in Aba. To the people of Akanu/Okpulor Autonomous Community, the nightmare associated with the poor state of the roads especially with the setting in of every RAINING SEASON is now a thing of the past. "We have not had it so good", the people retort.

The people of IBEME ANCIENT KINGDOM, Ward Eleven (11) should be ready as they are next in line to host this unprecedented phenomenal Programme. Ndewo nde k'anyi nu!! He oma edula anyi la aka!!!
