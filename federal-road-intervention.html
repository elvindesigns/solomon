INTERVENTION ON OSISIOMA/NNPC/ EKEAKPARA FEDERAL ROAD.

Right now, Hon Solomon Adaelu's Caterpillars and Bulldozers have been drafted on an intervention programme on Old Aba-Owerri road/Osisioma/ NNPC Federal road in order to ameliorate the long sufferings of the people.

the need for immediate intervention becomes necessary before the rain sets in. This will certainly bring back and reinvigorate the numerous abandoned industries and business enterprises that have gone comatose and as well improve the standard of living of the people of Aba.

All those people that ran away from Osisoma Industrial Zone due to flood and poor state of the roads should prepare to come back and start up their businesses once again.

Indeed, OBINGWA,OSISIOMA and UGWUNAGBO FEDERAL CONSTITUENCY is working!!

As the Rural Roads rehabilitation programme have been extended to Urban and FEDERAL Roads, we thank Hon Adaelu for coming to the aid of Aba people and by extension numerous Nigerians that will ply on this road upon completion. THANK YOU THE PEOPLE'S REP!! THANK YOU VOICE OF THE VOICELESS!!!
