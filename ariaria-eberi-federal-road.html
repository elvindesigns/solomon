INTERVENTION ON ARIARIA / EBERI-OMUMA FEDERAL ROAD

The people of Ariaria in Osisioma Ngwa LGA and Eberi-Omuma in Rivers State were today thrown into so much ecstasy and overflowing joy as the Ariaria/Eberi-Omuma Federal Road comes into the INTERVENTION of Hon Solomon Adaelu ROAD REHABILITATION and GRADING PROGAMME. This road which has not been attended to in any form for over twenty (20) years has put the residents into untold hardship. It cuts across several Communities and terminates at Asa-Oberete (on the side of Osisioma).

Over the years, we have been faced with plethora of unending Construction and Reconstruction work by NDDC and Federal Ministry of Works that only ended on paper and did not see the light of the day. Severally, funds meant for the road were disbursed and diverted into private pockets.

This particular road is so strategic to our people as the only means of conveying farm produce from the hinterland of Osisioma (Abia State) and Omuma (Rivers State) to Aba Urban. Aba being closer to Eberi-Omuma than Port-Harcourt makes the road so important for the two States. Rather than taking a long and tumultuous route through Umuojima and Umuode axis the people can now assess Aba directly from their homes and farms.

The perennial suffering and untold hardship encountered by our people due to the inaccessibility of this all-important road will be taken care of and the flood which always submerge buildings and farmlands at every RAINING SEASON will henceforth be a thing of the past. My Honourable, Nde ARIARIA ANCIENT KINGDOM and indeed OSISIOMA as a whole are highly elated and are saying, "imegbalahu" for coming to their AID!!! NDEWO nde k'anyi!!!
